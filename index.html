KWK '17

Kode with Klossy is a free
coding camp for young
women ages 13-18.

  • Tell Me More

Kode with Klossy was founded by Karlie Kloss
and located in 10 cities across the US.

All of the young women attending the camp will learn many skills and gain an insight into the world of code.

"Women are seriously under represented in the STEM fields."
-Karlie Kloss

Each of these camps lasts for 10 days from 9 a.m. to 5 p.m. Over the course of two weeks, the young women work together to develop different programs using the languages of code. Girls work in teams to collaborate while exploring the world of code.
