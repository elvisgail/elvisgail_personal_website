What is it like at KWK?

We're here, to tell you that your anxieties about camp are about nothing!

Coding Superpowers!

At Kode With Klossy you are not expected to know any coding. You will start out simple, using Ruby to make your computer say "Hello World." Your skills will be amazing by the end of the two weeks. By using HTML and CSS you will build your own simple, and complecated websites.

Why Is Coding Important?

Coding is used for EVERYTHING. Coding makes webpages, video games, machines, cars- EVERYTHING. Coding can change the world, and you should be a part of the, as I call it "Female Science Revoloution", to change the world. You'll feel like you can do anything.

FAQs- Lunch, TA's, Friends

Don't Worry. Everyone is accepting. If you're like me (the youngest of the bunch), you'll worry that the older girls will brush you aside. They will not. Kode With Klossy is a suportive community, and everyone cares about everyone.

The TAs and Teachers are the most helpful people ever. They want you to sucseed and know so much about coding, it's amazing. They will help you through all of the tasks, and lead you to an amzing website. They believe you can do it- We do, too.
